About Our Ministry

Called to Revive, Reach, and Raise a God-Fearing Generation

Revival Crusade Missions International (RCM) is a church dedicated to igniting spiritual revival, spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ, and raising a generation that walks in the fear of God. We believe in the transformative power of God's Word and are committed to discipling believers who will impact nations for Christ.

Our mission is to revive God’s people, reach the lost, and build a spiritually fervent, evangelistically potent, administratively sound, and socially relevant ministry. We are passionate about equipping believers to live in righteousness, serve with excellence, and worship in spirit and in truth.

Through evangelistic outreaches, discipleship programs, community impact, and kingdom service, we strive to see lives transformed, societies renewed, and nations prepared for the coming of the Lord.
